These are only some of the topics that interest me. The logo for the blog whoami is actually a reference to a common command in a linux kernel terminal emulator. The whoami outputs the user currently active in the terminal to the screen. To get a minimalistic and custom approach to blogging, I coded the entire website from scratch. Of course, I did use Bootstrap CSS for the navbar at the top of the page, but then again, that also took a big of customizing to match my preferences. On top of blogs about computer science, I also like to talk about the life of a student. I currently attend high school, and have lots to talk about the social hierarchies between the students, the faculty, and even sometimes between these two groups as well. I also discuss the difficulty of choice. We, as humans, make tons of choices everyday--but do we actually know why we make those choices. Follow me as I dive into the world of economics, statistics, and much more!
